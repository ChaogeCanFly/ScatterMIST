5.6.1 HEMI Integral

A HEMI integral calculates the reflectance over the entire hemisphere. It has no parameters, other than its name and incident polarization, and does not allow for any polarization sensitivity. The dialog box that opens when the user selects a HEMI integral is shown below:

Notice that, by default, the variable incidentpol is used as the incident polarization. Any Stokes vector may be substituted for incidentpol.
